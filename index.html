Welcome to Tazzina

Born from the legacy of Clay Oven Pizza, Tazzina is the new vision of three friends — Alessandro, Angus and Simon — bringing genuine Italian hospitality, wood-fired pizza, and traditional pasta to the heart of Greensborough.

The Forno

Hand-painted, brick-built, and burning every service. The soul of the kitchen.

Pizza Napoletana

Sicilian-rooted, Naples-trained. Soft crust, charred edges, made by Angus.

La cucina

Pasta fresca, Milanese classics, Sicilian arancini — Alessandro's nonna would approve.
